Project #2 Lost & Found

Description

My partner, Katie, lost a brown and green chessboard pattern scarf. I took the idea of the chessboard and try to draw it in p5.

Design Process

I first draw the first line of the whole drawing and then replace it into second line. These two lines form the basic pattern of the scarf then I use the loop function the fill the whole canvas. I tried to embeded push and pop function and acciedentally make the middle circle.

Reflection

In this project, I merged these functions to build a new pattern: push, pop, for loop, translate, rotate. It take me a while to think about how to connect those different function together. I think it's interesting to see how the computer works for me rather than copying the picutre in my mind.
