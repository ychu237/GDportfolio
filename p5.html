Project #5 Exquisite Corpse

Description

In this project, I exchange my corpse with Lu and Sunny. I have Sunny's head and lower body, Lu's body.

Design Process

In the beginning, I follow the steps provided by Lu and Sunny then I started to play with arguments and parameters. I also found some interesting rotate animation tutorial on the Internet and wanted to combined them into my design.

Reflection

It's interesting that both Lu and Sunny sharing the same idea of transformation. Both their characters are forced to become another form. I think anyone who involuntarily becomes another type will be very disturbed, so I want to present that awkward state. Between being able to move and not being able to move, I wanted to show the feeling of wanting to go back.
